What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you use an app or website. They help ensure functionality, security, and in some cases personalization of your experience.

Does SoulSync Use Cookies?

The SoulSync app is designed with privacy by design. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Only strictly necessary cookies may be used for:

  • Authentication: maintaining your session after login.
  • Security: protecting against fraud and abuse.
  • Preferences: remembering language or display settings.

Third-Party Cookies

We do not embed third-party advertising networks or analytics SDKs. Any cookies are first-party only and under our direct control.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies in your device or browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the App.

Legal Basis

Under GDPR, the use of strictly necessary cookies does not require consent. We rely on legitimate interest to provide secure and functional services. For any optional cookies (if added in the future), we will request explicit consent.
