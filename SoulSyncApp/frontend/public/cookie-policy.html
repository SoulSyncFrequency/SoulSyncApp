Does SoulSync Use Cookies?

The SoulSync app is designed with privacy by design. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Only strictly necessary cookies may be used for:

  • Authentication: maintaining your session after login.
  • Security: protecting against fraud and abuse.
  • Preferences: remembering language or display settings.

Third-Party Cookies

We do not embed third-party advertising networks or analytics SDKs. Any cookies are first-party only and under our direct control.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies in your device or browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the App.

Legal Basis

Under GDPR, the use of strictly necessary cookies does not require consent. We rely on legitimate interest to provide secure and functional services. For any optional cookies (if added in the future), we will request explicit consent.

Governing Law

This Policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of Croatia. For matters not expressly regulated, EU law (including GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive) applies.

Your GDPR Data Rights

As a user of SoulSync, you have the following rights regarding your personal data under the GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct or rectify inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict or object to processing of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at soulsyncfrequency@gmail.com.

Cookie Preferences and Retention

You can reject or withdraw consent to non-essential cookies at any time through your browser settings or a future Cookie Settings panel. You can also clear cookies manually from your browser. Necessary cookies last up to 12 months, analytics 6 months, marketing 3 months.
