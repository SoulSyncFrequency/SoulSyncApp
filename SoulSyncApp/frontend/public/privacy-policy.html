What We Collect

  • Account data: email and basic profile to sign in.
  • User input: questionnaire answers, direct text inputs, and settings.
  • Consent events: timestamps of privacy agreements and withdrawals (audit log).
  • Diagnostics: minimal technical logs necessary to operate the service (no third-party analytics).

How We Use Data

  • To provide personalized therapy outputs (nutrition plans, modules, PDFs).
  • To secure access (authentication, biometric/PIN unlock).
  • To prove consent and comply with legal obligations.

Where Data Is Stored

  • On-device: certain results and last exported PDFs may be stored locally using secure storage (Keychain/Keystore).
  • Our backend: account data and consent events may be stored on servers we control.

Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing. Outbound network requests from the backend are restricted via an allow-list.

Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Consent — for processing questionnaire inputs and generating outputs.
  • Contract — to provide the service you request.
  • Legitimate interests — to ensure security and prevent abuse.

Your Rights

  • Access, rectification, deletion
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time (does not affect prior processing)
  • Portability (export) of your data
  • Complaint to your local supervisory authority

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.

Security

We apply encryption in transit (HTTPS), secure on-device storage, PIN and biometric unlock, and least-privilege access controls.

Children

The App is not intended for children under 16. Do not use the App if you are under the applicable age in your jurisdiction.

Governing Law

Republic of Croatia law applies. For matters not expressly regulated, EU law (including GDPR) applies.

Changes

We may update this Policy. We will indicate the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, notify you in-app.

Your GDPR Data Rights

As a user of SoulSync, you have the following rights regarding your personal data under the GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct or rectify inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict or object to processing of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at soulsyncfrequency@gmail.com.

Service Providers and International Transfers

We use service providers (hosting, analytics, email) who process personal data on our behalf under signed Data Processing Agreements. If data is transferred outside the EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other safeguards.

Complaints

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority (for example, AZOP in Croatia) if you believe your data rights have been violated.
